    Features

    User Profiles :

    Influencer profiles with detailed information, including demographics, interests, and previous collaborations. Brands and marketers should also have profiles, specifying their target audience and campaign goals

    Search and Discovery :

    Advanced search filters to help brands discover influencers based on niche, location, engagement rates, and follower demographics. Recommendations and algorithms that suggest potential influencers for collaboration.

    Analytics and Tracking:

    Robust analytics to track the performance of influencer campaigns, including reach, engagement, and conversion metrics. Real-time tracking of campaign progress and influencer-generated content.

    Rating and Review System:

    A feedback system that allows both influencers and brands to rate and review each other after collaborations. Helps build trust and credibility within the community
